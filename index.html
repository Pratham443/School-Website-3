school activities

joy of giving

held in 2019

Soaking in the spirit of sharing and caring Podar International School, Hassan celebrated “Joy of Giving”. The Students took active part in the activities conducted in the classroom and contributed non perishable food items generously to help the needy. The Students visited nearby Government Higher Primary School and some needy families and donated the items. The activity presented the children with an opportunity to understand the joy of giving and sharing and develop social consciousness.

mathematics exhibition

held in 2019

Mathematics Exhibition was held at Podar International School, Hassan on 29th November, 2019. The students from grade 1 to 10 participated in the exhibition with wonderful models on various topics. The models were prepared under the guidance of their teachers. This exhibition gave them an opportunity to present their thoughts.
